## 🔍 How to Use

### Search Drinks
1. Click the search icon in the menu
2. Type drink name or ingredient
3. Results filter in real-time

### Browse by Category
1. Click "Beverages" in the category navigation
2. Scroll through all 150+ drinks
3. Click any drink card to flip and see details

### View Drink Details
1. Click on any drink card
2. Card flips to show description, allergens, calories
3. Click "Add to Order" button
